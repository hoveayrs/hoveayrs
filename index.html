Thys Clothing Project is my attempt to create items of clothing that make me feel naturally confident and comfortable.
They’re designs that combine my love of colour with significant feelings and memories from my life.
Each design represents a feeling that I experience. By wearing that symbol it allows me to understand and express that feeling in a different way.
It was important for me to design because i believe feeling comfortable is as much about feeling comfortable in your own skin as it wearing a comfortable clothing.
